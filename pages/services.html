Common Questions & Education

Empowering you to make informed decisions for your pet.

Is pet insurance worth it?

Absolutely. Insurance helps cover unexpected accidents and illnesses, ensuring your pet gets the best care without financial stress.

How often should I brush my pet's teeth?

Ideally, daily. Plaque can turn into tartar within 48 hours, so regular brushing is key to preventing dental disease.

What should I do in an emergency?

Keep calm and call our emergency line immediately. We'll provide first-aid advice while you're on your way to our clinic.

Are vaccinations safe?

Yes, vaccinations are a safe and effective way to protect your pet from life-threatening diseases. Side effects are rare and usually mild.
